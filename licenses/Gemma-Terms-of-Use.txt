What this means for this project
--------------------------------
- The Gemma WEIGHTS are NOT redistributed by Sovereign Agentic OS. They are
  pulled at runtime by the Ollama engine (`ollama pull gemma3n:e4b-it-q4_K_M`)
  from the Ollama model registry on first boot. We ship only the Ollama engine
  (MIT) and a Helm value that names the tag — hence the manifest marks the Gemma
  weights `bundled=no` (same treatment as other referenced-but-not-redistributed
  artifacts). The license gate therefore does NOT classify Gemma as Apache-2.0.

- An operator who runs the DEFAULT configuration WILL download and use Gemma
  weights under the Gemma Terms of Use. Anyone who needs a strictly Apache-2.0
  (or otherwise OSI-permissive) stack MUST override `modelServer.model` to a
  permissively-licensed tag (e.g. a Qwen Apache-2.0 tag, or Ministral) before
  deploying. See charts/sovereign-agentic-os/values.yaml (`modelServer.model`).

- The Gemma Terms permit use, reproduction, modification and distribution of the
  model and derivatives subject to the use restrictions in the Prohibited Use
  Policy and the requirement to pass through these terms and the use restrictions
  to downstream recipients. Read the canonical terms above before redistributing
  the weights yourself.
